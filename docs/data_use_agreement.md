# Data use agreement (restricted resources)

[![English](https://img.shields.io/badge/Language-English-blue.svg)](data_use_agreement.md) [![中文](https://img.shields.io/badge/语言-中文-red.svg)](data_use_agreement_zh.md)

This agreement applies to **restricted** mENACT v1.0.0 resources: participant audiovisual recordings (Zenodo R1–R3) and enactment scripts (Zenodo R4). The open neurophysiological and behavioral package (Zenodo R0) is public and not covered here, but citation requirements below apply whenever you use any mENACT resource.

**Before you request access:** read this page in full, then complete the steps in [access_request.md](access_request.md). On the [request form](https://forms.gle/UihkbfYnaaZjD8Cp7), you must check **every** box under **mENACT data use agreement (select all)**. Incomplete acknowledgment will not receive download links.

## 1. Scope

| Resource | Package | Contents |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Restricted AV | R1–R3 | Per-subject MP4 recordings (professional, amateur, and non-actor cohorts) |
| Restricted scripts | R4 | Enactment transcripts (`stimuli_exp3/*/*.txt`) |

Download links are sent by email after form submission. Links are personal and must not be shared (see §4).

## 2. Eligibility

Restricted files are released to researchers at universities, hospitals, or other research institutions who:

- Submit the access form with a valid **institutional email** (university or institute address where links will be sent);
- Describe a bona fide **research purpose** and name a **principal investigator or supervisor**;
- State **ethics / IRB status** for the *planned use* at their institution (approval ID and institution, or **N/A** with a brief explanation when no new human-subjects work is involved).

Submitting this form does **not** ask your IRB to approve the original mENACT study.

## 3. Permitted use

You may use restricted files **only** for **non-commercial academic research**, including education and scholarly publication, subject to:

- Your institution’s research-ethics rules and any approval required for your planned analysis or derivative work;
- Secure storage on systems you control or your institution provides;
- Compliance with all terms in this agreement.

## 4. Prohibited use

You must **not**:

- Use restricted files for commercial purposes or deliver them to third parties for commercial use;
- **Redistribute** restricted files (upload, publish, sell, or otherwise make them available to others);
- **Share** Zenodo secret download links or forward access emails;
- Attempt to **re-identify** participants or link recordings to individuals outside the dataset documentation;
- Use restricted files in ways that violate applicable law or your institution’s policies.

## 5. Citation and attribution

When you use restricted resources—or combine them with open R0 data or GitHub engineering files—you must:

- Cite the mENACT open dataset DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.20707429](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20707429);
- Cite the mENACT dataset paper when you publish work that relies on these files (use the bibliographic record for the IEEE TAFFC mENACT article once available).

Product DOIs and assembly steps: [downloads.md](downloads.md).

## 6. No redistribution of access

Access is granted to **you** (and your research group under your PI’s supervision) for the stated purpose. Colleagues who need the same files must submit their own request and agree to this document.

## 7. Changes and contact

The data team may update this agreement or revoke links if terms are violated. Questions or reports of misuse:

**Contact:** dzhang@tsinghua.edu.cn

## Form acknowledgment (select all)

On the request form, check **all four** boxes below. Wording matches the form exactly.

1. **Non-commercial academic research only; I follow my institution's ethics rules where required**  
   I will use restricted files only for non-commercial academic research and will obtain or document ethics approval at my institution when my planned use requires it.

2. **I will not redistribute restricted files or share download links**  
   I will keep files and Zenodo links within my authorized research context and will not pass them to others.

3. **I will not attempt to re-identify participants**  
   I will treat recordings and scripts as research data under participant consent and will not try to identify individuals.

4. **I will cite the mENACT dataset DOI and paper when using any resource**  
   I will cite DOI [10.5281/zenodo.20707429](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20707429) and the mENACT paper in publications that use these materials.
